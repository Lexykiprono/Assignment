About Period Poverty Awareness

Welcome to Period Poverty Awareness, a platform dedicated to shedding light on the challenges faced by individuals who struggle to access menstrual products and resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about period poverty and advocate for accessible menstrual products and education for everyone, regardless of their socioeconomic status.

What is Period Poverty?

Period poverty refers to the lack of access to menstrual products, proper sanitation facilities, and menstrual health education. It affects millions of individuals worldwide, leading to various health, social, and economic challenges.

Why We Care

We believe that menstruation is a natural and essential aspect of life, and no one should face barriers in managing their periods with dignity and comfort. Period poverty not only affects physical health but also impacts mental well-being and educational opportunities.

Our Goals

  • Advocate for policies that promote affordable and accessible menstrual products.
  • Provide educational resources about menstrual health and hygiene.
  • Support initiatives that empower individuals and communities affected by period poverty.
  • Collaborate with organizations and stakeholders to create sustainable solutions.

Get Involved

You can join us in our efforts to end period poverty by:

  • Donating menstrual products or funds to support our programs.
  • Sharing information and raising awareness on social media.
  • Volunteering your time and skills to help us expand our reach and impact.
  • Advocating for policy changes and supporting legislation that addresses period poverty.

Together, we can make a difference and ensure that menstruation is no longer a barrier to health, education, and dignity.
